## The Problem

Single parents face a housing affordability crisis:
- **40-60% of income** goes to rent in many markets
- Limited options for safe, family-friendly shared housing
- No trusted way to find verified, compatible co-living partners
- Traditional roommate platforms ignore child safety concerns

**Market Size:** 10.9M single-parent households in the US, 35% housing cost-burdened

---

## The Solution

CoNest is a **mobile platform** that connects verified single parents seeking shared housing. Our safety-first approach ensures every user passes background checks and ID verification before connecting.

**Key Features:**
- Mandatory ID verification + criminal background checks
- Compatibility matching algorithm (schedules, parenting style, house rules)
- End-to-end encrypted messaging
- Zero child data storage (parent-only profiles)

### SDVOSB Filing Requirements

**For SBA Veteran Small Business Certification:**
1. Service-connected disability rating (VA letter)
2. DD-214 (Certificate of Release/Discharge)
3. Proof of ownership (≥51% by service-disabled veteran)
4. Control documentation (veteran manages daily operations)
5. Business documentation (Articles, Operating Agreement, etc.)
6. SAM.gov registration (active UEI number)

**SBTDC can assist with:**

- SAM.gov registration
