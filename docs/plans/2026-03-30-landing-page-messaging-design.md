# Landing Page Messaging Redesign: Outcome-First Positioning

**Date**: 2026-03-30
**Status**: Approved
**Branch**: `placd-pivot`

---

## Executive Summary

Reposition the Placd landing page from tech-first ("intelligent matching") to outcome-first ("intake to placed in days, not weeks"). Research into housing placement org pain points revealed that matching is a differentiator but not the entry point — the daily pain is spreadsheet chaos, lost cases, and manual funder reporting. The pipeline is the front door; matching is the magic inside; compliance reporting is the lock-in.

**Core strategic shift**: Lead with outcome, prove with pipeline, differentiate with matching, lock in with compliance.

**Competitive positioning**: Placd is the missing workflow layer between HMIS (compliance data) and Salesforce (donor CRM). It doesn't replace either — it fills the gap where placements actually happen.

---

## Research Findings

### Industry Pain Points (ranked by frequency in sources)

1. **Spreadsheet-driven workflows** — Even agencies using HMIS run day-to-day placement in Excel. HMIS collects compliance data but doesn't manage workflow.
2. **No real-time visibility** — Program directors can't answer "how many families are we actively placing?" without asking each case manager individually.
3. **Manual funder reporting** — HUD, ORR, ESG reports compiled by hand from spreadsheets. Hours/days of work monthly.
4. **Disconnected systems** — Shelters, case managers, housing offices serve the same people without shared real-time data.
5. **Manual matching process** — Refugee resettlement agencies "match refugees to affiliates largely by hand, with staff meeting weekly in round-robin fashion." Pain is speed, not quality.

### Competitive Landscape

| | HMIS (Bitfocus, WellSky) | Salesforce Nonprofit | Spreadsheets | Placd |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Placement pipeline | No (compliance focus) | DIY customization | Sort of | Purpose-built |
| Client-unit matching | No | No | Manual | AI-powered |
| Compliance reports | Yes (generic) | DIY | Manual | One-click, housing-specific |
| Day-to-day workflow | Clunky | Generic | "Works" | Built for case managers |
| Price | $$$$ (enterprise) | $$$ + customization | Free | $499-999/mo |

**Primary competitor**: Spreadsheets and inertia — not software.

**Salesforce companion story**: "Salesforce tracks your organization. Placd tracks your placements — and proves your outcomes to funders."

## Section-by-Section Changes

### 1. Hero

**Current**:
- Eyebrow: "AI-Powered Placement"
- H1: "Place families faster with intelligent matching."
- Sub: "Six-factor scoring connects families to the right housing unit in minutes — not weeks. Compliance reporting built in from day one."
- CTAs: Request a Demo + Sign In

**Proposed**:
- Eyebrow: "Housing Placement Platform"
- H1: "From intake to placed — in days, not weeks."
- Sub: "Placd replaces spreadsheets with a purpose-built placement pipeline. Your team sees every case, AI surfaces the best-fit unit, and funder reports generate in one click."
- CTAs: Request a Demo + See How It Works (scroll to #how-it-works)

**Reasoning**: Declares category ownership. Leads with outcome buyers report to funders. Names spreadsheet replacement as the hook. Drops "Sign In" secondary CTA — first-time visitors don't have accounts.

### 2. Capabilities (flip the hierarchy)

**Current**: Matching (lead, 7-col) + Pipeline & Compliance (secondary, 5-col stacked)

**Proposed**: Pipeline (lead, 7-col) + Matching & Compliance (secondary, 5-col stacked)

**Lead card — Placement Pipeline** (7-col):
- Icon: Workflow
- H3: "Placement Pipeline"
- Body: "Visual kanban tracks every case from intake through move-in. Your team knows where every family stands — no spreadsheet cross-referencing, no cases falling through cracks."
- Visual: Six pipeline stages as horizontal flow: Intake → Matching → Proposed → Accepted → Placed → Closed

**Top secondary — Intelligent Matching** (5-col):
- Icon: GitMerge
- H3: "Intelligent Matching"
- Body: "AI scores every client-unit pair across six factors — location, budget, household size, language, accessibility, and services. Top matches surface automatically."

**Bottom secondary — One-Click Compliance** (5-col):
- Icon: LineChart
- H3: "One-Click Compliance"
- Body: "HUD- and ORR-ready reports generate instantly. Time-to-place, outcomes, demographics — always audit-ready, never assembled by hand."

**Reasoning**: Pipeline is the first thing you demo. It's tangible, visual, and immediately understood. Matching answers "why not Trello?" Compliance is the lock-in.

### 3. Impact → "What Changes"

**Rename section** from "Impact" to "What Changes" — pre-pilot, no impact data yet. Replace with directionally honest claims.

| Current | Proposed |
|---|---|
| "60%" — Faster time-to-place compared to manual matching workflows | **"Hours, not weeks"** — Time-to-match drops from manual spreadsheet review to instant AI-scored results |
| "6-factor" — AI scoring algorithm per client-unit pair for optimal placement | **"6 factors, 1 click"** — Location, budget, household, language, accessibility, and services — scored automatically for every client-unit pair |
| "100%" — Audit-ready compliance reporting from day one of deployment | **"Always audit-ready"** — HUD and ORR reports generate from live pipeline data — no end-of-month scramble |

**Reasoning**: No fabricated percentages. Directionally honest. Replace with real pilot numbers after first customer.

### 4. How It Works

**Keep 3-step structure. Tighten copy:**

**Step 1 — Intake**:
> "Case manager logs the client — household size, budget, language, location, accessibility needs. One form, structured data from day one."

**Step 2 — Match**:
> "Placd scores every available unit across six factors and surfaces the top matches. What took a week of phone calls happens in seconds."

**Step 3 — Place & Track**:
> "Case manager proposes a unit, the client accepts, and the placement moves through your pipeline — visible to your whole team until move-in and beyond."

**Reasoning**: Names the actor (case manager). Emphasizes speed contrast. Drops "AI" from this step (hero established it). "And beyond" signals ongoing outcomes tracking.

### 5. CTA Section

**Current**:
- H2: "Ready to modernize your placement workflow?"
- Body: "See Placd in action with a personalized demo tailored to your organization's needs."
- CTAs: Request a Demo + Try the Dashboard

**Proposed**:
- H2: "See what your placement workflow could look like."
- Body: "30-minute walkthrough with your org's data shape — not a generic slide deck. We'll show you intake to placed, with your caseload in mind."
- CTAs: Request a Demo + See How It Works

**Reasoning**: Specificity builds trust. "30 minutes" sets expectations. "Your data shape" signals customization without vague "tailored." Drop "Try the Dashboard" — demo-first, not self-serve.

### 6. Trust Strip

**Current**: `SOC 2 Type II · HIPAA Compliant · FedRAMP Ready · HUD Certified` (none held)

**Proposed**: `Veteran-Owned (SDVOSB in progress) · Built for HUD & ORR Compliance Standards · AES-256 Encryption`

**Reasoning**: All three claims are truthful. Veteran-owned is a procurement advantage for government-funded orgs. "Built for" is a design claim, not a certification. AES-256 is a technical fact in the codebase. Zero legal exposure.

### 7. Navigation & Footer

**Nav**: `Capabilities` → `Platform` · `Impact` → `What Changes` · `How it Works` (keep)

**Footer tagline**: "AI-Powered Housing Placement" → "Housing Placement Platform"

## Decisions Log

| Decision | Choice | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Lead message | Outcome-first, not tech-first | Buyers report time-to-place to funders, not algorithm sophistication |
| Hero capability | Pipeline over matching | Pipeline is demoed in 30 seconds; matching requires context to appreciate |
| Impact stats | Drop hard percentages | No pilot data yet; fabricated stats erode trust with grant-writing buyers |
| Trust strip | Remove uncertified claims | False compliance claims kill deals; replace with verifiable facts |
| Salesforce positioning | Companion, not competitor | "Salesforce tracks your org. Placd tracks your placements." |
| "AI" in hero | Removed from eyebrow, kept in sub | AI is a feature descriptor, not a category. Category = Housing Placement Platform |
| Secondary CTA | "See How It Works" over "Sign In" | First-time visitors don't have accounts; route deeper into page |
