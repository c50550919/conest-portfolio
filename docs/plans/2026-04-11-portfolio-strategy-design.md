## 1. Executive Summary

This strategy formally invalidates previous "immediate cash flow" models that attempted to commercialize `CodeDiligence.ai` or `Bear.pump` over the next 30 days. 

Based on the timelines established in the `2026-04-07-accelerator-pitch-strategy.md` file, the highest probability of outsized success lies in fully exploiting the Placd (B2B Housing SaaS) narrative, leveraging the SDVOSB moat, and targeting four immediate institutional accelerator deadlines.

All secondary projects are frozen to protect context-switching bandwidth.

## 3. Week-by-Week Execution

### Week 1: Submission Sprint (April 11 - 18)
* **Placd Priorities**:
  * Finalize 60-second and 3-minute pitch scripts.
  * Load scripts into Even Realities glasses. Practice 5x minimum.
  * **Submit Target 1**: PenFed Veteran Entrepreneur Accelerator
  * **Submit Target 2**: Google for Startups Black Founders Fund
  * **Submit Target 3**: Halcyon EquityTech Fellowship
  * Code Requirement: *Zero*. Rely on current demo screenshots/video.

### Week 2: The YC Push (April 19 - April 25)
* **Placd Priorities**:
  * Build the Y Combinator application text offline.
  * Record the 1-minute video. Use the Even Realities teleprompter. One take is fine. Optionally inject a 5-second screen share of the Placd Kanban match view.
  * Code Requirement: Only touch the Next.js `web/` folder if a bug actively breaks the 5-second video demo. Do not build new routes.

### Week 3: Refinement & Review (April 26 - May 2)
* **Placd Priorities**:
  * Solicit external feedback on YC application answers.
  * Refine the narrative based on any feedback from early PenFed/Halcyon submissions if received.
* **Job Search**: Double application volume to hedge May/June outcomes.

### Week 4: Final Submission (May 3 - May 9)
* **May 4**: Submit YC Application (deadline 8PM PT).
* **May 5-8**: Attend PenFed Tysons cohort (if accepted).

## 4. Success Metrics & Kill Criteria

### Success Metrics
1. 100% submission rate: PenFed, Google BFF, Halcyon, and YC submitted before deadlines.
2. 5+ practice sessions completed using Even Realities hardware before the YC video recording.
3. 1+ acceptance into an accelerator or fellowship program.

### The "June 15" Kill Criteria & Re-Pivot
This approach intentionally sacrifices short-term cash flow (via CodeDiligence) for institutional backing and a massive narrative moat via Placd.

**If YC, Google, Halcyon, and PenFed all issue rejections by June 15**:
1. Conclude the Accelerator Sprint.
2. Re-evaluate startup runway based on the FTE job search results.
3. Immediately unfreeze `CodeDiligence.ai` and execute "Approach 3: Revenue Pivot" (manual VC audits) to generate cash flow.
