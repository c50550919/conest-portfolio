## Business Description

Placd is B2B SaaS for housing placement organizations — nonprofits, refugee resettlement agencies, housing authorities, and employer-assisted housing programs. These organizations place families in homes manually: spreadsheets, email chains, gut-feel matching. Placd replaces that with intelligent matching (weighted scoring on budget, language, accessibility, household size, location), a placement pipeline, and compliance reporting.

## Veteran Connection

I'm a disabled Air Force veteran. I understand institutional housing because I lived the military housing assignment process — being matched to base housing based on rank, family size, and availability is the same workflow these organizations run, just without software. My service-disabled veteran status qualifies me for SDVOSB certification, which opens set-aside government procurement for housing authorities and VA programs. This is a structural competitive advantage that no VC-backed competitor can replicate.

## MVP Status

Working demo with real matching engine — not mockups. Multi-tenant architecture with org-scoped authentication, encrypted sensitive data, and configurable matching weights. Placement pipeline (kanban with drag-and-drop), client roster with intake forms, compliance reporting (PDF export), and role-based access control for case managers, program directors, and org admins. Built solo as a full-stack security engineer.

## Scalability

10,000+ housing nonprofits, 3,300+ housing authorities, 350+ refugee resettlement offices in the U.S. Same matching engine serves corporate relocation, insurance ALE, military housing, and senior living — each a new vertical without rebuilding the product. SaaS model ($499-$999/month + $50/placement) scales with org count and placement volume.
