## YC Interview Rapid-Fire Q&A

```
Q: What do you make?
A: Placement matching software for housing orgs.

Q: Revenue?
A: Pre-revenue. Working demo. In discovery.

Q: How do you get customers?
A: Direct outreach to Charlotte housing orgs. 5 in pipeline.

Q: Why will they switch?
A: Not switching FROM anything. No tool exists. Switching from spreadsheets and email.

Q: What if Salesforce builds this?
A: They've had 20 years to add matching to Nonprofit Cloud. Haven't.

Q: What if HMIS vendors add matching?
A: HMIS = compliance reporting. Adding matching changes their product category. We're native.

Q: Why you?
A: Built 2 platforms solo. Security engineer. SDVOSB access to 3,300+ housing authorities.

Q: Biggest risk?
A: Sales cycle. Nonprofits are slow. Mitigating: dual lane — corporate (Atrium, fast) + nonprofit (CRRA, mission).

Q: How big can this get?
A: Housing alone = $50M+ TAM. Add insurance ALE + military + senior living = $500M+.

Q: Why now?
A: HMIS has had data for 10 years with no matching. Policy pressure + housing crisis = orgs need efficiency NOW.
```
