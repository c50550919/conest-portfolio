## Grant vs. Equity Trade-offs

### Why Pursue Grants First?

**Advantages**:
- ✅ $0 equity dilution (keep ownership)
- ✅ Validates concept with third-party endorsement
- ✅ De-risks for future investors (better valuation)
- ✅ Longer runway before needing equity capital
- ✅ Forces discipline on metrics and outcomes

**Disadvantages**:
- ⏰ Competitive (10-50% acceptance rates)
- ⏰ Slower process (3-6 month cycles)
- ⏰ Bureaucratic (reporting requirements)
- 💰 Smaller amounts ($50K-$250K vs. $500K-$2M)

### When to Raise Equity?

**Right Time**:
- ✅ After proving concept with grants
- ✅ After hitting 100+ placements
- ✅ After achieving $10K+ MRR
- ✅ When you need $500K+ for growth
- ✅ When grants alone won't get you to next milestone

**Wrong Time**:
- ❌ Before proving product-market fit
- ❌ When grants can fund next 6-12 months
- ❌ When valuation will be significantly higher in 6 months
- ❌ Before exhausting non-dilutive options
